1. Who we are

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how Aurex Digital (“we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, stores, discloses, and protects personal data when you use:

  • the Anchor mobile application for Android (“App”);
  • our customer portal API at https://api.aurexdigital.in (“API”); and
  • this website where this Policy is published (“Website”).

2. Scope and applicability

This Policy applies to borrowers and authorised users who access the App using credentials linked to a vehicle finance agreement.

We aim to comply with applicable Indian laws, including:

  • the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (“DPDP Act”);
  • the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 (“SPDI Rules”); and
  • other applicable regulations relating to digital lending, consumer protection, and telecommunications.

Where your finance company’s loan agreement or privacy notice imposes additional obligations, those documents also apply to the extent they govern your relationship with that finance company.

3. Definitions

  • Personal data means any data about an identifiable individual.
  • Sensitive personal data may include financial information and precise geolocation, as classified under applicable law.
  • Finance company means the non-banking financial company, lender, or financier that originated or services your vehicle loan and uses the Aurex Finance Desk platform.
  • Data principal means you, the individual to whom the personal data relates.
  • Data fiduciary means the entity that determines the purpose and means of processing. Depending on context, your finance company and/or Aurex Digital may act as a data fiduciary.
  • Data processor means an entity that processes personal data on behalf of a data fiduciary. Aurex Digital may act as a processor for finance companies in respect of borrower data stored and transmitted through our platform.

4. Roles: data fiduciary and processor

Anchor is a technology platform that connects borrowers with their finance companies. In many cases:

  • your finance company is the primary party responsible for your loan relationship and may be the data fiduciary for loan and borrower records; and
  • Aurex Digital provides the App, API, hosting, and related infrastructure, and may process data as a data fiduciary for App operations or as a data processor acting on instructions of finance companies.

For questions about how your finance company uses your data, contact them directly. For App-specific privacy questions, contact us using the details in Section 20.

5. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the App and the permissions you grant, we may collect the following categories of information.

5.1 Identity and loan access credentials

  • Vehicle registration number
  • Loan agreement / reference number
  • Session authentication token issued after successful login

5.2 Personal and profile information

  • Borrower name
  • Agreement identifier
  • Finance company name
  • Vehicle description / label

5.3 Financial and loan information

  • Loan amount, interest rate, interest method, tenure, and status
  • EMI schedule (due dates, amounts, paid amounts, overdue status)
  • Payment history (amounts, dates, receipt numbers, payment types)
  • Outstanding balance and summary figures shown on your dashboard
  • Loan documents made available to you through the App (file names, types, download metadata)
  • Payment initiation details (amount, method selected, installment number)

5.4 Location information

  • Precise GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
  • Location accuracy (in metres), where available
  • Timestamps associated with location transmissions

Location is collected only after you grant permission and consent in the App. See Section 8.

5.5 Device, app, and technical information

  • Device operating system version and device manufacturer/model (where available through system APIs)
  • App version
  • Network connectivity status
  • IP address and request metadata in server logs
  • Crash, diagnostic, and performance data if we enable analytics or error reporting in future releases (we will update this Policy if we do)
  • Locally stored preferences such as terms acceptance, location consent status, active loan session, notification reminder state, and background setup completion flags

5.6 Communications and notifications

  • EMI reminder notification content generated on-device based on your loan due dates
  • Foreground service notification shown while background location services are active

5.7 Information we do not intentionally collect

We do not require access to your contacts, photos, microphone, calendar, SMS content, or call logs for core App functionality. We do not sell your personal data.

6. How we collect information

  • Directly from you when you sign in, accept terms, grant permissions, initiate payments, or interact with the App.
  • From your finance company via the API when your credentials are validated and dashboard data is retrieved.
  • Automatically when the App transmits location updates, syncs in the background, sends heartbeat signals, or communicates with our servers.
  • From your device through Android permissions you approve (location, notifications, battery optimisation exemption requests, and device location/GPS state).

7. Legal bases and consent

We process personal data based on one or more of the following grounds, as applicable under Indian law:

  • Consent — e.g. when you accept this Policy and Terms, and when you explicitly allow location access and background location.
  • Performance of contract — to provide App features linked to your finance agreement.
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable laws, court orders, or regulatory requests.
  • Legitimate uses — as permitted under the DPDP Act, such as preventing fraud, ensuring network security, and maintaining service integrity, where applicable.

You may withdraw consent for optional processing (such as background location) through device settings and in-app controls. Withdrawal may limit or disable features. Some processing may continue where required by law or your loan agreement.

8. Location data (important)

Background location is a core feature of this App. If you grant consent, the App may collect your device’s precise location while the App is in use and while it is in the background or closed, subject to Android permissions and device settings.

8.1 When location is collected

  • After you sign in and accept the Terms and this Policy
  • After you explicitly grant location permissions through Android and in-app prompts
  • While a foreground location service is running
  • Periodically via scheduled background sync and heartbeat mechanisms
  • When significant movement is detected (e.g. beyond a configured distance threshold)

8.2 Frequency

Location collection is periodic, not continuous streaming. Depending on device state, movement, battery optimisations, and network availability, updates may occur at intervals ranging from approximately hourly to every few hours, and when material movement is detected. If device GPS/location is turned off, collection may pause until location is re-enabled.

8.3 Purpose of location collection

  • To allow your finance company to monitor the location of financed vehicles
  • To support asset security, recovery, and verification of service continuity linked to your loan
  • To display tracking-related information to authorised finance company personnel in the Finance Desk platform
  • To detect prolonged gaps in location reporting and trigger follow-up under your finance company’s policies

8.4 Who receives location data

Location data is transmitted to our API and made available to the finance company associated with your agreement and authorised Aurex Digital personnel who require access for support, security, and system administration on a need-to-know basis.

8.5 Your controls

  • You may decline location permission; some features may be restricted.
  • You can change permissions in Android Settings at any time.
  • Revoking background location may cause the App to limit functionality or display setup prompts.
  • Uninstalling the App stops future collection from that device.

8.6 Prominent disclosure

Before background location permission is requested, the App presents an in-app explanation of what is collected, why it is needed, and that data is shared with your finance company. This Policy supplements that disclosure.

9. How we use information

We use personal data to:

  • authenticate you and maintain your session across one or more linked loans on a device;
  • display loan summaries, EMI schedules, payment history, and documents;
  • initiate and record payment requests and display receipts;
  • collect and transmit location data as described in Section 8;
  • send EMI due reminders and service notifications;
  • maintain background services, including restart after reboot where permitted;
  • monitor API security, troubleshoot errors, and prevent abuse;
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms;
  • improve the App and develop new features in accordance with applicable law.

10. How we share information

We may share personal data with:

10.1 Your finance company

The lender or financier that holds your agreement receives loan-related data and location data necessary for servicing and monitoring under your agreement.

10.2 Service providers and infrastructure partners

We use third parties to host, secure, and operate the platform, which may include cloud hosting providers, database services, networking/CDN providers, and email or SMS gateways if enabled. These providers process data only to perform services for us under contractual confidentiality and security obligations.

10.3 Payment processors

If you make a payment through a payment gateway, UPI intent, or checkout link, payment data is handled by the relevant payment service provider in accordance with its terms and PCI/UPI applicable standards. We do not store full payment card details on our servers unless explicitly stated otherwise in the payment flow.

10.4 Legal and safety disclosures

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of users, finance companies, Aurex Digital, or the public.

10.5 Business transfers

If we undergo a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, personal data may be transferred subject to this Policy or a successor policy with notice as required by law.

10.6 What we do not do

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data to third-party advertisers.
  • We do not share location data with unrelated third parties for their independent marketing.

11. Payments

When you initiate a payment through the App, we transmit payment instructions to our API and, where applicable, redirect you to third-party payment interfaces. Payment partners may collect identifiers, transaction amounts, and device/network metadata necessary to process the transaction. Review the payment provider’s privacy notice before completing payment.

12. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer period is required or permitted by law or your loan agreement.

  • Session tokens — for the duration of your authenticated session and as configured on our servers.
  • Location history — for the period required by your finance company’s policies and applicable law; finance companies may retain location records in Finance Desk according to their retention schedules.
  • Loan and payment records — typically for the life of the agreement and statutory limitation / regulatory periods thereafter.
  • Server logs — for a limited period for security and debugging, unless extended for incident investigation.
  • On-device data — until you clear app data or uninstall the App.

When data is no longer required, we take reasonable steps to delete, anonymise, or aggregate it.

13. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational safeguards designed to protect personal data, including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for data in transit between the App and API;
  • access controls and authentication for API endpoints;
  • restricted access to production systems on a need-to-know basis;
  • secure development and deployment practices.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. You are responsible for keeping your device secure and not sharing your agreement credentials with unauthorised persons.

If we become aware of a personal data breach likely to affect your rights, we will notify affected parties and authorities as required by applicable law.

14. Your rights

Subject to applicable law (including the DPDP Act), you may have the right to:

  • Access personal data we hold about you;
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • Erasure of data where retention is no longer necessary and no legal exception applies;
  • Withdraw consent for processing based on consent;
  • Nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity, as permitted by law;
  • Grievance redressal through our Grievance Officer (Section 20).

To exercise rights relating to loan records, we may direct you to your finance company where they are the data fiduciary. We will assist as required by law.

We may need to verify your identity before responding. We aim to respond within timelines prescribed by applicable law.

15. Children

The App is intended for adults who have entered into or are party to a vehicle finance agreement. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

16. International transfers

Our primary systems are intended to be hosted in India. If data is processed or stored outside India, we will do so in compliance with applicable cross-border transfer requirements under Indian law and with appropriate safeguards.

17. Third-party links and services

18. Automated decision-making

We do not make solely automated decisions that produce significant legal effects about you without human involvement, except where permitted by law. Finance companies may apply their own risk or collections policies outside the App.
